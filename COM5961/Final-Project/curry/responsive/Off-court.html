Off-court Life


  • A Sportspersons Family

  • Born in Akron and grown up in Charlotte, Stephen Curry is the son of Sonya and Dell Curry, a former volleyball player and professional basketball player in NBA.
    Stephen Curry has a younger brother, Seth Curry, who is also a NBA professional basketball player. When they were young, their father took them to NBA games and started to train them.
    Stephen's younger sister, Sydel Curry is also an American former college volleyball player as her mother.

  • Marriage and Kids

  • Curry first met Ayesha in a church youth group, when they were 15 and 14 years old respectively. Years later, the two reconnected when Ayesha was pursuing her acting career in Hollywood and Stephen was visiting for an awards show. Soon after, they began dating.
    In 2011, Stephen Curry married his longtime girlfriend Ayesha Alexander.
    Together, they have three children, daughters Riley, and Ryan, and son Canon.
