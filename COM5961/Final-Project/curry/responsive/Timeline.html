2009

On June 25, 2009, Curry was selected with the seventh overall pick in the 2009 NBA draft by the Golden State Warriors.

He finished his rookie season with 166 three-pointers, the most ever by a rookie in NBA history.

2010

In the sophomore year, Curry led the free throw percentage of .934 in the league, and scored 18.6 points on average.

2011

In Feburary, Stepehen Curry won the Skills Challenge during the All-Star Weekend.
In May, he was received the NBA Sportsman Award with his ethical behavior, fair play, and integrity on the court.

2012

In the sophomore year, Curry led the three throw percentage of .934 in the league, and scored 18.6 points on average.
But his ankli injuries resulted in his absence of over 40 games.

2013

Curry's 54 points against the Knicks included a career-best and franchise-record 11 three-pointers, becoming the first player in NBA history to score 50-plus points while hitting 10-plus three-pointers in a game.

2014

Scoring 24 points on average and making 261 three-pointers, Curry led the league in thress in back-to-back season.

2015

Leading GSW with 67 wins, Curry was voted the NBA MVP.
For the finals, Curry averaged 26 points and won the Cleveland Cavaliers in 4-2.

2016

A crazy season for Stephen Curry with 402 three-pointers and MVP again.
A buzzer beater half-court three pointer from Curry helped defeated the Thunder on Feb 27, and reached the NBA three-pointers single game record.

2017

Curry helped the Warriors clinch the series against the Cleveland Cavaliers and the championship in Game 5 with 34 points, as Golden State claimed its second title in three years.

2018

In Game 2 of the NBA Finals, Curry hit a Finals-record nine 3-pointers and became the career finals learers for 3-pointers.
Curry and GSW won the third championship in four years since the season of 2014-2015.

2019

On April 14, with 8 3-pointers Curry made against the Clippers, he became the career playoffs leaders for 3-pointers.
Unfortunately, Curry and GSW missed their fourth championship during the loss in the finals against the Raptors.
